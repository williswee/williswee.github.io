I founded Tech in Asia (TIA) in 2010 (unofficially in 2009) with a mission to build and serve Asia's tech and startup community. It was one of the most meaningful and fulfilling things I've ever done.

I got to work with brilliant people and investors (East Ventures, Y Combinator, SoftBank, Eduardo Saverin, and more), travel across the world, and help shape the region's startup ecosystem. I'm so grateful for the opportunity to have been a part of it.

In my job at TIA, I stayed closest to the product and business side of things, which I really enjoyed. I've always loved bridging the gap between beautiful, usable products and a viable business model.

After an incredible 16-year journey, I stepped down as CEO on 31 March 2026. It was a bittersweet moment. I'm immensely proud of what we've built and deeply grateful for our talented, dedicated team. I'm now taking time off to recharge with my family before embarking on my next adventure.
