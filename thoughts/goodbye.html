Thank you and goodbye, Tech in Asia

31 March 2026

After 16 years of building Tech in Asia, it's officially time for me to graduate. My last day will be March 31.

The integration with SPH Media is moving into its next ops phase, which means it's the perfect time for me to get out of the way.

I'm incredibly proud of what this team has built, and I couldn't be happier to hand the keys over to Terence Lee. He knows the TIA community inside out, and the ship is in the best possible hands. (Eh, no pressure, Terence).

I'm not disappearing completely just yet. I'll be sticking around as an external advisor for the next 6 months. Mostly to cheerlead from the sidelines and occasionally offer unsolicited opinions.

As for me, I'm looking forward to taking a proper break, catching up on sleep, play more tennis, read more books, and spending time with my fam before figuring out what to build next.

To everyone who has read, partnered, or worked with us over the last 16 years... thank you. It's been a wild ride, and your support has meant the world to me. 🙏❤️
