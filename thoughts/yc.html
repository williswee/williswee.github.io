Joining Y Combinator

4 February 2015

Tech in Asia is part of the current Y Combinator Winter 2015 batch and I'm learning a ton.

Through YC, I get to understand why certain things in our company have worked really well and why certain things haven't. We will continue to make something people want and stay true to our mission.

The last thing we want is complacency. As Kevin Hale of YC said at our last Startup Asia Tokyo conference:

[It’s] easy and dangerous to get complacent in other regions, where you might think 'I’m the best startup in this location.' You [then] don’t focus on being innovative, or being agile.
